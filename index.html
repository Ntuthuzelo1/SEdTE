Social Educational Technology Environment (PTY) LTD

A private company with one founding partner, currently operating in the digital space by providing a digital service through an application and advertising through a website.

  • It seeks to digitalize the learning of our languages.
  • Committed to quality instructional education targeting all social media platforms with the objective of deliverying environmentally friendly programs (applications).
  • It is a company consisting of individuals with a joined experience of more than a decade in the spaace of entrepreneurship specialising in different fields such as marketing, project coordinating, implementation of policies, developing mobile applications websites and perfoming technical operations in the space of technicians

Key objectives for SEdTE. (PTY) LTD in the first year as follows:

  • Establish a legal business through appropriate licensing
  • Promote culture and the learning of languages
  • Complete business planning and pursue funding-via venture capital, banking loans, grants, and contracts.
  • Establish a web presence, dominate the social media space

Mission statements

  • SEdTE. (PTY) LTD designs, develops tech related solutions which seeks to uplift the learning of our home languages.
  • It seeks to provide a flexible environmentally friendly e-learning environment, fulfilling e-learning operational needs.
  • Its principal goal is Innovation, impacting lives and advancing with technology as it advances.

Customer service delivery is at the core of the company's priorities. The company operates on a for-profit basis and provides an engaging and equitable work environment for its staff and future contractors.

Success will be dependent upon:

  • Persistent and creative customer development efforts
  • Exceptional service and product quality
  • Time-efficient and cost-effective development processes
  • Expert management and knowledgeable staff
  • Growth strategies
